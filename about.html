What is the purpose of the organisation?

CABSA helps and motivates the Church to bring a huge change in the areas of well-being,
health and gender equality, in a world affected by HIV.

Christian communities that are able to bring a solution and more hope in the states of health,
well-being and equality in a world affected by HIV.
Optimistic Christian Communities that are able to bring light in the darkness,
hope to the hopeless and help out where they can.



CABSA was founded in 2001 with a goal to assist faith communities to respond to the HIV Epidemic.
Since there is quite a variety of numbers in every part of Africa,
CABSA believes a magnificent potential exists for churches
and followers of Jesus to be part of the solution to the HIV issue.

To achieve this, CABSA encourages and helps the Church to bring a change in the areas of gender equality,
health and many more, in a world affected by HIV.
